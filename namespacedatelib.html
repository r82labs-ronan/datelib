Adjust a date according to a business day convention.

Parameters
dateThe date to adjust
conventionThe business day convention to apply
calendarThe holiday calendar to use for checking business days
weekend_daysThe set of weekdays considered as weekend (defaults to Saturday and Sunday)
Returns
The adjusted date according to the specified convention
Exceptions
std::invalid_argumentif the input date is invalid

This function adjusts non-business days according to market conventions:

  • Following: Moves to the next business day
  • ModifiedFollowing: Moves to the next business day unless it crosses into a new month; if it does, moves to the previous business day
  • Preceding: Moves to the previous business day
  • ModifiedPreceding: Moves to the previous business day unless it crosses into a new month; if it does, moves to the next business day
  • Unadjusted: Returns the date unchanged
